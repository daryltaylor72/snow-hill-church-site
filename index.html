Welcome Home

Snow Hill Missionary Baptist Church is a Christ‑centered, Bible‑teaching church family serving Roseboro and the surrounding communities. We are committed to worship, discipleship, and loving our neighbors through prayer, service, and outreach.

Whether you are new to faith or looking for a church home, you are welcome here. Come as you are — there’s a place for you.
